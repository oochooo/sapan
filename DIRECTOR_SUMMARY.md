## Executive Summary

### What is Sapan.io?

Sapan.io is Thailand's first data-driven startup ecosystem enablement platform. It connects **Founders** with **Mentors** through:
- Profile creation with industry-specific tags
- Filtered discovery and browsing
- Connection request system with approval workflow
